About Us

We are a deep-tech MedTech startup on a mission to transform neonatal and pediatric health through advanced smart biosensors, machine learning, and real-time monitoring systems. Our flagship innovation—Smart NICU—brings AI-powered insights and thermal-vision analytics for critical care of newborns and preterm babies.

What We Build

Smart NICU Platform

End-to-end integrated neonatal health monitoring powered by thermal imaging, edge AI, and real-time alerts.

Fetal Monitoring Sensors

Non-invasive wearable biosensors for continuous fetal heart rate and movement monitoring.

Early Diagnostic AI

Predictive analytics for early detection of neonatal sepsis, respiratory distress, and neuro-developmental risks.
