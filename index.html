The Problem

Neonatal Jaundice is a common yet critical condition in preterm babies. If untreated, it can lead to severe complications such as kernicterus and sepsis. In low-resource areas like rural India and parts of Africa, the lack of affordable diagnostic tools makes effective treatment challenging.

Our Solution

The MarsuNeo Bilirubinometer is a device under development that uses multi-wavelength light analysis and deep neural networks to non-invasively and accurately measure bilirubin levels through the skin.
